# CatchTales Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 2026*

## Information We Collect

CatchTales collects the following information to provide and improve our fishing companion service:

- **Account Information**: Email address and display name (when you create an account)
- **Catch Data**: Species, weight, length, location (GPS coordinates), date/time, weather conditions, and photos you upload
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, and app version for diagnostics
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app to improve features

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide, maintain, and improve our services
- To enable cloud sync and Fish Together features
- To send Pro license codes and support communications
- To diagnose technical issues
- To generate anonymous community statistics

## Data Storage & Security

- Your catch data is stored locally on your device and optionally in Firebase Cloud Firestore
- Photos are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage
- We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase** (Google) — authentication, database, storage, analytics
- **OpenWeatherMap** — weather data and forecasts
- **OpenStreetMap / OpenSeaMap** — map tiles and nautical data
- **Stripe** — payment processing for Pro upgrades

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Anonymized, aggregated catch statistics may be shared through the Community Stats feature.

## Your Rights

- Access, update, or delete your data at any time
- Export your data in CSV, JSON, or KML format
- Withdraw consent for cloud features at any time
