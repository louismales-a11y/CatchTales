All Features

Everything you need to fish smarter and brag bigger.

🐟

AI Fish ID AI POWERED

Snap a photo of your catch and AI identifies the species instantly. No more guessing — just accurate identification from a clear photo.

🎤

Voice Recording

Log catches completely hands-free. Say "caught a 5lb largemouth bass at the dock with a spinner" and the form fills itself. Perfect for when your hands are busy with fish and gear.

🌤️

Weather & Forecast

Real-time weather conditions and a 5-day forecast. Temperature, humidity, wind speed, and conditions — all auto-fetched from your GPS location when you log a catch.

🌙

Solunar Best Fishing Times

Daily solunar predictions with major and minor fishing periods. Moon phase, illumination, age, and a 0-10 daily rating. Know exactly when the fish are biting.

🗺️

Interactive Map

Every catch plotted on an interactive map with species pins. Nautical charts, depth contours, weather overlays — all at your fingertips. Save your favourite spots and log depth readings.

🏆

Brag Board

Share your catch photos with the community. Like, comment, and show off your biggest fish. Built-in abuse reporting keeps it friendly.

📊

Statistics & Badges

Detailed statistics with charts, species breakdown, personal records, and achievement badges. Share your stats as an image. Track your progress as an angler.

🤖

AI Lake Insights AI POWERED

Ask your catch data anything: "What's my best lure for pike?" or "Where do I catch the most fish?" AI analyzes your personal data and gives you answers.

🎣

AI Tackle Recommendations AI POWERED

Get smart lure suggestions based on species, weather conditions, season, and water type. AI picks the best tackle for your specific situation.

🎯

Tackle Box & Catalog

Catalog your lures, hooks, lines, and baits. Browse 30+ lure types with target species. Weather-aware "Today's Pick" recommends what to use right now.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Back up your catches to the cloud, sync across devices, and fish together with friends in real-time sessions. Chat, share locations, and send emergency alerts.

📅

Calendar & History

View your catch history on a calendar heatmap. Browse photos in gallery view. Search, filter, and export your data to CSV, JSON, or KML.

🤳

Selfie Camera

Built-in selfie camera with a 3-2-1 countdown. Perfect catch photos every time — even when you're fishing solo.

🌊

Nautical Charts

Buoys, wrecks, depth contours, and water temperature. Plan your fishing spots with professional-grade marine data.
